Food ordering application

Design and PoC implementation

DCT Studio is a hair salon in Örnsködsvik and when they opened their business they were in the need of a website. They contacted me and asked if I could help them with their website, and of course, my answer was yes.

We started talking about what they needed and wanted on the website and what some of the general stylings would be. But they were not sure what they wanted except for information about their website and some pictures so they gave me a pretty wide range to work with.

The first thing I did was to look for a color scheme and font that would fit the rest of the feel and impression of the website, and after some talking with the customer I found a color scheme that they were really happy with. After that, I started with making some lo-fi designs and after some iterations both me and the customer were happy with how the information would be displayed.

Then the implementation started and it went pretty well. No major problems occurred and the time frame the customer had given me was held. When the implementation was done the customer also asked if I could help them figure out how to host the website and connect the website with their domain name. This was a challenge for me because it was the first time I did this but after some time I figured it out and got the website up and running.

This was a very special project for me because it was the first time I worked with a customer outside of school, and to see my webpage be used by a real company was an amazing feeling.
