About Me

My Passion Is Creating Websites

My name is Linus Lundqvist and I am 21 years old and am currently studying in my third year of the Master of Science Programme in Interaction Technology and Design at Umeå University.

Before I started my studies at University I studied “Naturvetenskap med inriktning Natur” at upper secondary school/high school, and worked different kinds of jobs during summer breaks and during my free time. Some of these include working at a supermarket, warehouse work at “Naturkompaniet” and as a host for an event company. My work at the event company included taking care of groups of people when they performed activities like paintball and other team-oriented competitions. I have also done a lot of volunteering at several big outside sport events in my hometown.

During my studies have I also experienced how it is to be a project leader several times and have also taken a course in project Project management.
After (and during) my studies my dream work would be somewhere where I can use my skills as a UX/UI Designer and front-end developer to grow the company I work at as well as myself.
